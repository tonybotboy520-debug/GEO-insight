**EN**

Well, at least in comparison to our initial study , it indicates that Google is selecting far fewer pages straight from the original SERP (~76% in July 2025 vs. ~38% today). To me, that suggests AI Overviews are relying less on the direct search results and more on the sources showing up in fan out query SERPs. Google has confirmed that its system performs a “Query fan-out” whenever a user searches and AI is triggered. This is when the initial query is split into multiple related sub-queries. The pages that appear most often within those sub-query SERPs then get cited in the AI Overview.

**中文**

好吧，至少与我们最初的研究相比，它表明 Google 直接从原始 SERP 中选择的页面要少得多（2025 年 7 月约为 76%，而今天约为 38%）。对我来说，这表明人工智能概述较少依赖于直接搜索结果，而更多地依赖于扇出查询 SERP 中显示的来源。谷歌已确认，每当用户搜索并触发人工智能时，其系统都会执行“查询扇出”。这是初始查询被拆分为多个相关子查询的情况。然后，在这些子查询 SERP 中最常出现的页面会在 AI 概述中被引用。
