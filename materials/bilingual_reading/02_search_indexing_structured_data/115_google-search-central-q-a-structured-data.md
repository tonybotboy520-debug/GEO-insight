### 段落 12

**EN**

For content that represents a question and its answers, you can mark up your data with the schema.org QAPage , Question , and Answer types. Properly marked up pages are eligible to have a rich result displayed on the search results page. This rich treatment helps your site reach the right users on Search. For example, you might see a rich result for the user query "How do I remove a cable that is stuck in a USB port?" if the page has been marked up with answers to that question. In addition to enabling your content for the rich result treatment, marking up your Q&A page helps Google generate a better snippet for your page.

**中文**

对于代表问题及其答案的内容，您可以使用 schema.org QAPage、 Question 和 Answer 类型来标记数据。正确标记的页面有资格在搜索结果页面上显示丰富的结果。这种丰富的处理方式可以帮助您的网站在搜索中吸引到合适的用户。例如，您可能会看到用户查询“如何移除卡在 USB 端口中的电缆？”的丰富结果。如果该页面已标记有该问题的答案。除了使您的内容能够进行富结果处理之外，标记您的问答页面还可以帮助 Google 为您的页面生成更好的代码片段。
